MONASH YOUNG MEDTECH INNOVATORS
Dedicated to furthering positive impact through medical technology and healthcare innovation.
Who we are.

An interdisciplinary community of undergraduates, PhDs and Early Career Researchers (ECRs) dedicated to achieving positive impact through medical technology and biomedical innovation.

Based at Monash University, founded in November 2018. Open to all, regardless of home institution, discipline, creed or experience.

Our Vision.

To connect young people with academia, industry and community, and drive innovation in healthcare and medical technology

Our Mission.

Build Australia’s largest youth-led innovation ecosystem for interdisciplinary and grassroots approaches to healthcare by 2021.

What we're like.

We’re empathetic.

  • We listen to understand, and take time to put ourselves in other people's positions.

We’re inclusive.

  • We're all different and we're all equal. In the end, we build bridges not walls.
  • No one should be made to feel unvalued.

We’re effective.

  • We make things happen. Even if at the start, we're not sure exactly how.
  • We're dependable, resourceful and entrepreneurial.
  • Our standards are very high, and we work very hard to to live up to them.

We're all in this together.

  • We're supportive of each other. When the going gets tough, we reach out to lend a hand.
  • We will make mistakes, and we'll learn from them. It'll turn out alright in the end. We forgive ourselves and others.

We're here for a good time.

  • Life is ridiculous, short, and full of twists and turns, so we don't take ourselves too seriously.
  • We're stupid in a good way, foolish in an even better way.
  • We're here, we're alive and we're here to stay, so why not enjoy it.
